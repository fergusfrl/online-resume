About Me

Getting into the outdoors - pushing myself both physically and mentally has always been - and will continue to be a mantra for my life.

Recently University study and part time jobs have been keeping me indoors but that is an environment where I still strive for excellence and focus on improving and challenging myself.

I love making new friends and meeting new people both professionally and socially. I work well in a team and am happy to take a leading role - a position outdoors pursuits have equipped me for well.
